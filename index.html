Research Interests

    Vineet Kumar is a faculty member at Yale School of Management, and an expert on Digital Technologies. His focus areas include networks, large scale data analytics (big data), and digital products and services. He studies how firms should create and deliver value drivers in digital products and services, and strategies firms should adopt in developing and marketing digital products.
